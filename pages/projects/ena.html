ENA: Dream BBQ

ENA Dream BBQ is a game by Joel Guerra and the ENA Team. released march 26, 2025, it currently sits at over 22,000 positive reviews on steam

consultant:

i was consulted on many things for the project including but not limited to; level flow, puzzles, supporter edition content, music, sound, character dialogue and more

photos:

many photos of mine were used as textures and as pause screen effects. provided is a vidoe showcasing all of the pause screen photos for an example. All of mine have [WalrusDog] in the file name.

video credit to [adamdev2]

playtesting:

i was a major playtester and worked with the team to polish and streamline the game as much as possible. this included providing tons of feedback, finding and replicating bugs and providing new ideas for areas and levels.

sound:

parts of my voice were used in the audio of the game. the human board stepping sounds are one i know of but some of the others i still do not know.
